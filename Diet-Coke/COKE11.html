Our History

Diet Coke was introduced in 1982 as a bold new step in the evolution of refreshment. It was the first extension of the Coca-Cola brand and quickly became one of the most successful beverage launches in history.

Unlike traditional cola, Diet Coke was crafted with a unique formula — lighter, sharper, and designed for those who wanted full flavor without sugar.

Over the decades, Diet Coke has become a cultural icon, representing confidence, style, and modern attitude across the globe.
